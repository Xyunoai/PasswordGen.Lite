Password Safety Class

Introduction

Passwords are keys to your online world. Protect your accounts and personal info.

  • Protect personal info (emails, photos, messages)
  • Keep online reputation safe
  • Prevent hackers from stealing your identity
Example: Someone logging into your account could harm your friendships.
Activity: List your accounts and check for weak passwords.

Risks of Weak Passwords

  • Common passwords: 123456, password, qwerty
  • Using same password for multiple accounts is risky
Example: A hacked social media account can spread false info.

Strong Password Characteristics

  • At least 12 characters
  • Include uppercase, lowercase, numbers, symbols
  • Avoid personal info
Example: Weak: dog123 | Strong: D0g!Run$Fast12
