Password Checklist

This checklist helps you learn good password habits. Nothing you enter is saved or sent online.

  • Use a unique password for each account
  • Use a password manager to store passwords
  • Include uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols
  • Avoid common words, names, or dates
  • Make passwords at least 12 characters long
  • Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) when possible
